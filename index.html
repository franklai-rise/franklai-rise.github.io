Selected Research

Master's Thesis Ongoing

Raft-type Wave Energy Converter Using Decommissioned GFRP Blades

Proposed a novel solution to the global wind blade waste problem by repurposing them into wave energy converters (WEC). The research is structured into three distinct phases.

1

Material Characterization

Analyzed damage mechanisms in service-aged GFRP materials using Micro-CT scanning and Instron tensile testing to determine residual mechanical properties.
