Master's Thesis | Ongoing

Raft-type Wave Energy Converter Using Decommissioned GFRP Blades

Proposed a novel solution to the global wind blade waste problem by repurposing them into wave energy converters (WEC).

  • Material Characterization: Utilized Micro-CT and Instron to analyze service-aged GFRP damage mechanisms.
  • Statistical Modeling: Established a stochastic damage model using Weibull distribution (SCI Paper Under Review).
  • System Design: Designing GFRP-Steel connections using Abaqus/ANSYS and planning wave tank tests.
